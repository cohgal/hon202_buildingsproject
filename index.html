Who the hell is Stephen Epler?!

The founder of Portland State University, Stephen Epler was born in Brooklyn, Iowa, in 1909.

He went on to attain a master’s degree in education from the University of Nebraska and teach high school science in Chester, Nebraska.

In 1936, Epler moved to New York to begin doctoral studies. After attaining a Ph.D. in educational sociology and accepting a position as the dean of men at Southern Oregon College in Ashland, Oregon in 1940, he joined the U.S. Navy in 1943.
